# HRDesk Leave Policy

## Vacation Days

All full-time employees are entitled to **25 vacation days** per calendar year. Part-time employees receive a prorated amount based on their contracted hours.

Unused vacation days may be carried over to the following year, up to a maximum of **5 days**. Any balance beyond that is forfeited on January 1st.

Employees are encouraged to take at least 15 vacation days per year for their wellbeing.

## Sick Leave

Employees may take up to **10 paid sick days** per year. Sick leave is not charged against the vacation balance.

For absences longer than 3 consecutive days, a medical certificate must be submitted to HR within 5 working days.

## Parental Leave

- **Maternity leave:** 14 weeks fully paid
- **Paternity leave:** 4 weeks fully paid
- **Shared parental leave:** up to 12 additional weeks, split between parents

Parental leave requests should be submitted at least 60 days before the expected start date.

## Public Holidays

HRDesk observes all national public holidays. A full list is published each January in the employee portal.

## How to Request Leave

All leave requests are submitted through the self-service HR portal. Approvals usually take 1-2 business days. For urgent requests, contact your manager directly.
